Option 1 · Grouped Navigator

What changes: the same items, but sorted under 3 plain headings — Page (the parts of your page), Design (global look), Settings. One neutral accent color, no rainbow.

Feels like: Vercel / Linear settings. Clean, calm, familiar.

Effort: lower. Still a flat list — you don't reorder the page from here.

Option 2 · Section List ★ recommended

What changes: the left panel becomes the actual map of your page — a list of sections you can drag to reorder, hide, or click to edit. "Design" and "Settings" sit behind the top switch.

Feels like: Shopify / Beacons / Linktree — what people now expect from a site builder.

Effort: higher. But it's the same idea for all 4 editors (linkinbio blocks = main/single sections = payment's single form).
