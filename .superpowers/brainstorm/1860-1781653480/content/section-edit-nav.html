This is the #1 decision for heavy editing. The right panel (live preview) stays put in all three — we're deciding what the LEFT panel does on click.

A · Drill-in (master → detail) ★ recommended

The section list slides away and the section's full form takes the whole left panel, with a "‹ Back to sections" header. One section at a time, all the room for fields, preview fully visible.

Best when: sections have many fields (your case). Shopify, Beacons, iOS Settings all do this.

B · Inline accordion

Click a section and it expands in place, pushing the others down. You keep the whole list in view.

Catch: with lots of fields the expanded form gets very tall and you're constantly scrolling past collapsed sections. Good for 2–4 fields, awkward for heavy editing.

C · Right drawer over preview

Click a section → a panel slides in from the right, over the preview. The section list stays visible on the left.

Catch: the drawer covers the very preview the creator is trying to watch change as they type. Fights your live-preview setup.
